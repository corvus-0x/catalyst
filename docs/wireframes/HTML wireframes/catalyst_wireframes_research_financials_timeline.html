Shortcut: jumps directly to that finding without going through left panel

Filters timeline to show only events connected to that entity; other events dim

Zoom in / Fit all buttons
Collapses/expands date groupings — "Fit all" shows all years; "Zoom in" shows one year at a time with expanded event cards
